# MindMate 🧠

AI-powered campus friend-matching app. Find your people.

## The Problem

Dating apps exist. LinkedIn exists. But there's nothing for students who just want to find their people — someone to build with, run with, or just vibe with. Most students have no idea who else on their campus shares their interests.

## What it does

You create a profile — what you study, what you do in your free time, what you're building, what kind of person you're looking for. The AI reads your profile and picks the 3 most compatible people from real registered users on campus, with a specific personal reason for each match. Not just "you both like music" — actually why you'd click.

## Features

- Create and edit your profile
- AI matching powered by Groq (LLaMA 3.3 70B)
- Send and receive connection requests
- Accept or decline incoming requests
- View all your connections
- Persistent accounts with Supabase

## Tech Stack

- React + Vite
- Groq API (LLaMA 3.3 70B)
- Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- Vercel (deployment)
